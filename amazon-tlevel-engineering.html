Introduction to Engineering

The Engineering T-Level is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the engineering sector, equipping them with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in a variety of engineering roles.

About Engineering at Amazon

At Amazon, we are committed to fostering innovation and excellence in engineering. Our T-Level program offers students the opportunity to gain hands-on experience, learn from industry experts, and develop the skills necessary for a successful career in engineering.

What You'll Do

During your T-Level, you will engage in a variety of activities designed to enhance your understanding of engineering principles and practices. You will work on real-world projects, collaborate with professionals, and develop the skills needed to succeed in the engineering sector.

  • Participate in hands-on engineering projects
  • Learn about different engineering disciplines
  • Develop problem-solving and critical thinking skills
  • Gain insights into the latest engineering technologies and practices
  • Collaborate with industry professionals and peers

Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for the Engineering T-Level program at Amazon, students should meet the following criteria:

  • Be 18+ before the start date (September 2026)
  • Have the right to live and work in England for the programme duration
  • Have lived in the UK/EEA for the last 3 years
  • Be an UK resident for the full duration
  • Not be registered on another government-funded course ending August 2026 or later
  • Have at least 5 GCSEs at grade 4–9 (A–C) including Maths and English
  • Not already hold a qualification in a similar subject at the same time or higher level

Programme Details

Duration - 18 Months
Apprenticeship Level - Level 3 Engineer
Environment - Corporate office/Operations site
Working Hours - 40 hours per week
Learning Format - Mix of on-the-job training, online study, and mentoring
Start Date - September 2026

Who We're Looking For

We are looking for motivated individuals who are passionate about engineering and eager to learn. Ideal candidates will have a strong academic background, excellent problem-solving skills, and the ability to work collaboratively in a team environment.

You'll thrive if you:

  • Strong academic performance in STEM subjects
  • Demonstrated interest in engineering through projects or extracurricular activities
  • Excellent communication and teamwork skills
  • Ability to think critically and solve complex problems
  • Willingness to learn and adapt in a fast-paced environment

Future Development

Upon successful completion of the T-Level program, graduates may have the opportunity to pursue further education or training in engineering, as well as potential career advancement within Amazon or the wider industry.
