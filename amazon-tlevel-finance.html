Introduction To Finance

As a Finance Apprentice at Amazon, you'll build strong foundations in accounting, analysis, and financial decision-making while supporting teams that keep our business moving. You'll learn how financial performance is measured, how budgets are created, and how data informs the choices leaders make every day. This programme is perfect for someone who is analytical, organised, and eager to learn how finance works in a fast-paced, real-world environment.

About Finance At Amazon

As a Finance Apprentice, you'll join Amazon as a Financial Analyst and rotate across three different placements over the course of your programme. These rotations expose you to
a variety of finance disciplines including Accounting, Finance, and Tax, giving you a broad and well-rounded understanding of how financial decisions are made in a global organisation.
Your rotations could take place in teams across many areas of Amazon, such as AWS, Retail, Fresh, Prime Video, or other corporate and operational business lines. Every team operates
differently, giving you the chance to explore a range of financial processes and business models. While each placement varies, all roles require analytical thinking, attention to detail, and
the ability to communicate financial insights clearly. You'll work in a fast-paced environment, supporting financial processes, helping interpret data, and contributing to decisions that
impact customers and business performance. Throughout your apprenticeship, you'll combine technical learning with hands-on experience, gaining the confidence and expertise to grow
into a professional finance career.

What You'll Do

During your rotations, you'll support financial processes that help leaders make informed, sustainable decisions. You'll analyse complex data, prepare reports, and collaborate with
teams across the business. You'll help shape financial performance by interpreting trends, supporting forecasting, and providing insights that guide strategic decision-making.

During Your Apprenticeship, You'll Learn How To:

  • Apply core accounting principles to real business scenarios
  • Assist with budgeting and financial forecasting
  • Analyse data and produce accurate financial reports
  • Work with financial software and databases
  • Build strong working relationships with teams and stakeholders
  • Understand regulatory and ethical considerations in accoutancy
  • Support taxation activities and understand their impact on the business

Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for this apprenticeship, you must:

  • Be 18+ before the start date (September 2026)
  • Have the right to live and work in England for the programme duration
  • Have lived in the UK/EEA for the last 3 years
  • Be an UK resident for the full duration
  • Not be registered on another government-funded course ending August 2026 or later
  • Hold 3 A Levels at grades BBC (or equivilent)
  • Not already hold a qualification in a similar subject at the same time or higher level
  • Provide a UK ENIC Statement of Comparibility if you studied internationally

Who We're Looking For

We welcome applicants from all backgrounds who have a genuine interest in finance and business.

You'll thrive if you:

  • Enjoy working with numbers and solving analytical problems
