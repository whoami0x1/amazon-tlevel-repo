Introduction to Business

The Business T-Level program provides a comprehensive introduction to the world of business, covering key concepts such as marketing, finance, and operations here at Amazon.

About Business at Amazon

At Amazon, we are committed to fostering a culture of innovation and excellence in business education. Our T-Level program is designed to provide students with real-world experience and insights from industry leaders.

What to Expect

Students can expect a dynamic learning environment that combines theoretical knowledge with practical application. Our program includes hands-on projects, industry partnerships, and opportunities for networking and mentorship.

During your T-Level, you will:

  • Engage in real-world business projects
  • Develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Learn from experienced professionals in the field
  • Gain insights into various business functions and industries
  • Build a strong foundation for future career opportunities

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for the Business T-Level program at Amazon, students must meet the following criteria:

  • Be 18+ before the start date (September 2026)
  • Have the right to live and work in England for the programme duration
  • Have lived in the UK/EEA for the last 3 years
  • Be an UK resident for the full duration
  • Not be registered on another government-funded course ending August 2026 or later
  • Have at least 5 GCSEs at grade 4–9 (A–C) including Maths and English
  • Not already hold a qualification in a similar subject at the same time or higher level

Programme Details

Duration - 18 months
Level - Level 3 Business Administrator
Environment - Corporate office/Operations site
Working Hours - 40 hours per week
Learning Format - Mix of on-the job training, online study, and mentoring
Start Date - September 2026

Who We're Looking For

We welcome applicants from all backgrounds who have a genuine interest in business and a desire to learn.

You'll thrive if you:

  • Are passionate about business and eager to develop your skills
  • Have strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Are a team player who can collaborate effectively with others
  • Can think critically and solve problems creatively
  • Are organised and able to manage your time effectively
  • Are open to feedback and willing to learn from others

You do not need prior administrative experience, just the motivation to learn and grow.

Future Development

Completing the Business T-Level opens doors across Amazon and the wider business industry. You could progress into roles such as Business Analyst or Operations Manager. With practical experience and a strong foundation in business principles, you'll be well-equipped to take on new challenges and advance your career.
