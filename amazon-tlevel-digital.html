What To Expect

In the Digital T-Level program, students can expect a hands-on learning experience that combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills. The curriculum is designed to be engaging and relevant, preparing students for the demands of the digital workforce.

During you'r T-Level, you will:

  • Engage in real-world digital projects
  • Develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Learn from experienced professionals in the field
  • Gain insights into various digital technologies and trends
  • Build a strong foundation for future career opportunities

Eligibility Criteria

To enroll in the Digital T-Level program, students should meet the following criteria:

  • Be 18+ before the start date (September 2026)
  • Have the right to live and work in England for the programme duration
  • Have lived in the UK/EEA for the last 3 years
  • Be an UK resident for the full duration
  • Not be registered on another government-funded course ending August 2026 or later
  • Have at least 5 GCSEs at grade 4–9 (A–C) including Maths and English
  • Not already hold a qualification in a similar subject at the same time or higher level

Who We're Looking For

We are seeking motivated individuals who are passionate about technology and eager to learn. Ideal candidates will have a strong academic background, excellent communication skills, and a desire to succeed in the digital world.

You'll enjoy this T-Level if you are:

  • Enjoy solving problems and thinking logically
  • Are curious about how software works
  • Communicate clearly and enjoy collaborating with others
  • Have strong attention to detail
  • Are motivated to learn new skills and improve continuously
